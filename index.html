Meet our Team of Experts



Dr. Gurneen Sidhu

I grew up in the Maritimes and always loved school. I actually entered the profession quite young being one of the youngest in my class (Dalhousie Dentistry 1996!) to graduate. I moved to Ottawa from my hometown in Dartmouth N.S.and spent the next 15 years practicing as an associate dentist. Aside from family dentistry, I have also had the opportunity to work for the city of Ottawa’s dental clinics, provide dental service to retirement homes, work as a clinical instructor in Algonquin’s hygiene program, and work for the Royal College of Dental Surgeons where I presently hold a committee position. I practice general and preventative dentistry. My aim is always to educate my patients. I think being the daughter of two teachers and having two sisters as teachers means I couldn’t do it any other way! I enjoy working in a relaxed and efficient atmosphere because it means I can focus on my patients.

Dr. Julie Delcorde

Dr. Julie Delcorde was born and raised in Ottawa. She graduated summa cum laude from the University of Ottawa with a Bachelor of Science in Biochemistry in 2012. With the assistance of the NSERC Alexander Graham Bell Canada Graduate Scholarship, she continued her graduate studies in Biochemistry at the University of Ottawa working in labs at the National Research Council of Canada and The Scripps Research Institute in California. Dr. Delcorde's research on host-viral interactions during viral pathogenesis has been published in numerous scientific journals. Dr. Delcorde completed her Doctorate in Dental Surgery at the University of Toronto where she received the prestigious Lynda Sellars Award in Comprehensive Patient Care. Dr. Delcorde served as the President of the Federation of Canadian Dentistry Student Associations (FCDSA) and organized the first national Canadian Dental Student Conference which hosted over 650 dental students from across Canada.
